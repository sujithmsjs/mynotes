ON TIME:
I hopes our train leaves on time.
If it doesn't leave on time, we could miss our connecting train.
The electrician said he would be here at 12.
He's right on time.

Students must submit their applications on time. Late applications will not be accepted.

IN TIME: Before it is too late.

in time to + verb
in time for + noun

I always reach my home in time to put my kids to bed.
I don't know if I'll get everything done in time for the wedding.
Sorry, I can't join you for breakfast. I have to be at the office in time to meet the first client.
